About us

Welcome to Cinema SHHILENCE, a place where stories come to life and dreams are projected on the big screen. We are more than a movie theater; we are a community passionate about the art of cinema, committed to providing unforgettable cinematic experiences for each of our visitors. From our humble beginnings, we have believed in the transformative power of cinema. Here at Cinema SHHILENCE, every film is an open door to new worlds, deep emotions and moments that stay with us long after the lights go up. We strive to select a diverse and rich programming that ranges from the most anticipated blockbusters to the gems of independent cinema to the classics that never go out of style. We pride ourselves on providing warm and attentive service. We believe that a night at the movies at home should be a complete experience, which is why our service is non-ticketed. We want to thank you for being part of our story and for allowing us to be part of yours. Every laugh, every tear and every sigh shared in our theaters reminds us why we love what we do.

Thank you for choosing Cinema SHHILENCE, where cinema is lived from the heart.

See you at the next screening!
